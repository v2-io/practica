## 1. Two different objects wear the name "practica / OPERATA"

**PRINCIPIA-OPERATA** is an *entity's own* efforts / priorities / objectives: a single agent's strategy substate $G_t=(O_t,\Sigma_t)$. **LOCUS-practica** is a *project's* "hierarchical and meaningful graph of ongoing efforts": a **composite agent system** ( #scope-composite-agent, Section III) — the advanced case, serial AI sub-agents under total context turnover **plus at least one human**, a heterogeneous composite whose sub-agents never co-exist. These are not the same *kind* of object: the difference is architecture-class plus a construction requirement (§3 — specifically the W₁ structural separation, the thing that makes a project a single trustworthy composite rather than a pile of self-reports).

The engineering named this distinction before the theory did, and it is stronger told straight. `~/src/operata/`'s own tracking notes (Dec 2025, predating the theory) state it explicitly — *"Operata serves two distinct but related purposes that will likely be independent systems with a sync layer, not a shared database … Previous sessions conflated these as 'two views of one database' when they're actually two independent systems that sync,"* and then name the exact downstream symptoms of the conflation (Perspective ambiguity, `owner_id` vs `locus_id`, the storage model). The theory's contribution at §1 is therefore not discovery but **grounding**: AAT supplies the architecture-class reason the engineering's distinction is real ( #scope-composite-agent: a set is a composite only under teleological alignment, else $O_c$ is ill-defined and "the composite is a fiction"). The convergence of an independent engineering effort and a later theory on the *same* split is itself the evidence (§9).

## 2. The forced single-agent spine

Verified at source; forced for *any* actuated agent in scope; practica may not violate these without breaking principle.

**State, not action; action derived each cycle.** #der-recursive-update (*exact, partly definitional* — its Epistemic Status verbatim: temporal-ordering and partial-observability do genuine eliminative work; state-completeness is definitional): carry recursive compressed state, not history. #der-action-selection (*exact* within Section I scope): action is a function of state, never stored. This is operata's core inversion, *forced not chosen* — and the one the engineering independently built and then hit the limits of (§9).

**Acyclic, directed effort / intent graph.** #deriv-graph-structure-uniqueness: directedness + **acyclicity** *proved* (temporal order over a finite horizon); Markov factorization *proved under causal sufficiency* (the Causal Markov Condition theorem). The must-be-a-DAG **necessity is open** in a specific sense — the segment is *Cox-in-form but not Cox-in-strength*: Cox's theorem is *necessary-and-sufficient* (probability is the **only** measure satisfying its desiderata — a uniqueness result), whereas this segment is *sufficiency-only* — the operational desiderata **guarantee** a DAG-with-Markov representation, but no one has shown a non-DAG structure *cannot* satisfy them. So "the strategy must be a DAG" should be read as "must-if-sufficient-via-this-route," not as proved necessity. The AND/OR edge parameterization is **convergent-choice** (every tried alternative failed or recovered it), not derived.

**Orient-cascade order is forced.** #der-orient-cascade (*ordering exact*, by information dependency): model-update $\to$ attainability $\to$ regret $\to$ strategy $\to$ objective. The *ordering* — objective-revision is the last step — is exact. **What is not exact, stated at its true tier:** that a self-actuating composite's root objective must be revised against a *non-objective* terminal invariant is **not "provably the last resort."** It is a *conditional, scoped no-go* ( #deriv-self-actuation-grounding, `status: conditional`): explicitly *not* the unscoped "no such object exists" (the universal-over-all-$\Phi$ step is argued, not derived), resting on three named premises (scalar-objective scope; no primitive reflective oracle; the #der-directed-separation substrate). What *is* exact is the constructive boundary's canonical instance: the persistence bound ( #result-persistence-condition, *exact*) is a non-objective terminal invariant the self-actuation operator structurally cannot reach. So, at the right tiers: the cascade ordering is exact; *that revision must ground on a non-objective invariant* is the conditional/scoped no-go; *the persistence bound is such an invariant* is exact. For a project the terminal invariant is the charter / persistence floor; the tool must not let the goal be the thing rewritten when work is hard — asserted at the no-go's tier, not stronger.

## 3. The trust lever — directed separation, class-coercion, W₁

*This is the most mechanism-heavy lever. If you need only the finding: **practica must structurally separate the goal-blind belief-update from the goal-conditioned strategy-update — the "strict" regime (W₁ below); anything weaker is a trust hole, not a style choice.** Take the bolded directives and proceed; return to the bridge-lemma paragraph only to evaluate the composite-cost claim.*

An individual LLM sub-agent is **GUC Class 3 (Coupled)** — #der-directed-separation (*conditional*): directed separation *fails by construction*; the goal is causally upstream of belief-update (the system prompt carrying $O_t$ sits first, so attention conditions every later computation on it — #def-coupled-update-dynamics, *robust-qualitative*). This is the formal name of the self-justifying-tracker failure and of the posturing failure: an agent whose belief-update is goal-conditioned tells you what serves the goal, not what is.

#der-class-coercion-via-wrapping (*conditional* on C1–C3; Theorem 1 exact, Theorem 2 = a KL-leakage bound): a Class-3 component embedded in an external scaffold whose **belief-store is updated only by goal-blind queries** and whose **goal-store is updated separately** is **Class 1 (Separated) by construction** — trustworthy *structurally*, not by the agent's good behavior. The segment names this, in its own words, a **truthification mechanism** — the rigorous formal version of what peer review, prediction registers, and double-entry bookkeeping do informally. **practica is that scaffold.** The theorem-forced build invariant: the store recording *the state of the work* (what landed, what failed, what is true) must be updated through a path that *structurally cannot take the current objective as input*, separate from the store recording *what we are trying to do*.

**The three wrapping regimes — name each by what it *is*; the W-index runs inverse to the strength, so do not read it as a number line.** **W₀ — no wrapping** (the un-truthified base; bare markdown with no constraint — the 38-open / 1-done PRACTICA rot is the W₀ failure). **W₂ — behavioral wrapping** (one goal-conditioned call, response parsed into typed fields; bounded only *behaviorally* by the component's compliance — *no structural bound*). **W₁ — structural wrapping** (separate goal-blind and goal-conditioned calls; bounded *structurally*). Strength is therefore **none ≪ behavioral ≪ structural** (i.e. W₀ ≪ W₂ ≪ W₁); **the structural regime, W₁, is the target.** #der-logogenic-as-wrapping (*conditional*): LLMs are Class-B (admit both a goal-blind mode — "extract the facts in this observation" — and a goal-conditioned mode), so they are wrappable; PROPRIUM-as-implemented and shoshin are at the behavioral (W₂) regime; the **auxilia hierarchy** (the entity's *own internal sub-agents* — extensions of its mind that share its core identity, not external tools) is the candidate structural (W₁) realization: cheap goal-blind sub-agent calls update the belief store, the main goal-conditioned call updates strategy. **practica should target the structural (W₁) regime**, and the auxilia pattern is how. The realistic regime is W₂-with-empirically-characterized-$\kappa$ (LLM-specific leakage: pretraining co-occurrence, RLHF instruction-following bias, system-prompt contamination, few-shot leakage), so exact W₁ needs care — but the *direction* is structural, not behavioral.

**A second, independent pre-theory witness for this lever (operata, §9).** operata's Dec-2025 engineering research independently arrived at "the CLI as a deterministic plumbing layer that separates *what to do* (agent intelligence) from *when/whether* (deterministic substrate)," and its taxonomy split tooling into deterministic / linguistic / reasoning / high-order degrees. That is the W₁ goal-blind-substrate / goal-conditioned-intelligence separation, reached from engineering first principles before AAT existed — convergence on the trust lever, from two directions.

**The composite cost (W₁ is not free).** #der-class-coercion-in-composition (*conditional*, "Tier-1 clean" — meaning clean for the Tier-1 belief-update class defined in the bridge-lemma paragraph just below; if you have not read that yet, read "Tier-1" as "Bayesian / gradient belief-update, the realistic case"): the wrapped system is a valid AAT composite (it satisfies (A1)–(A4) of #form-composition-closure under wrapper-design constraints, automatic for Tier-1 belief-update maps), inherits the sector-persistence template, and pays a Brooks's-Law tempo cost — W₁ needs $K\geq2$ component calls per macro-step, so wrapper tempo is the underlying rate divided by $K$: **$\geq2\times$ agent-calls per unit of progress** versus W₂'s $K=1$. Two closure quantities the segment explicitly warns not to conflate: $\varepsilon^\ast_{\text{track}}$ (does the ledger faithfully track project reality — must stay small) versus $\varepsilon^\ast_{\text{coerce}}$ (how far the wrapper deliberately bends the agent off its native self-report — practica *wants* this $\gt0$; it *is* the truthification). A practica that minimized $\varepsilon^\ast_{\text{coerce}}$ would defeat its purpose; one that let $\varepsilon^\ast_{\text{track}}$ grow is a lying ledger.

**The bridge-lemma condition (the hinge from closure-defect to bounded trajectory).** In order: the bridge from "closure defect" to "trajectory stays bounded" needs a condition *strictly stronger* than the one-point sector bound (A4) — the **incremental sector bound DA′a-inc** (strong monotonicity of the macro-correction). DA′a-inc is *proved strictly stronger than (A4)* by an explicit counterexample (oscillatory globally-inward but locally-non-monotone corrections). There is a structural-transparency equivalence — *DA′-inc $\equiv$ the contraction-metric condition (CT2) at the identity metric $M=I$* — itself `status: exact` (standard monotone-operator theory), which makes the Euclidean cases **AAT-internally derived, not theorem-imported**. The tier then splits cleanly: **Tier 1** (Bayesian updaters on exponential families, gradient on strongly-convex losses, linear-PD) — the bridge lemma is *derived*; **Tier 2** (nonlinear prediction) — local, factor degraded by $\kappa(D\hat o)^2$; **Tier 3** (non-convex / discontinuous) — verify per domain. **Net for practica:** if the wrapper's belief-update is Bayesian or gradient (the realistic choice), the contraction hinge is **derived**; only exotic belief-update maps drop to Tier-3. The honest residual is the precise tempo *number* — #der-tempo-composition is `status: sketch`, so the coordination-overhead number is shape, not certified — **not** the *existence* of the persistence guarantee, which is Tier-1 derived.

**Composite persistence has two distinct failure modes** ( #form-composition-closure, CM4 = contraction $\wedge$ scope-satisfaction): the composite **fails to contract** (coordination overhead outruns correction — Brooks) *or* it **was never a composite** (teleological unity lost, $O_c$ ill-defined). A practica that only watched throughput would miss the second entirely. Composite (A4) is *verifiable from sub-agent parameters* (weakest-link: $\alpha_c\geq\min_i(\alpha_i-\Delta\mathcal T_i^{\text{cost}})$, $R_c\leq\min_i R_i$) — you can check composite persistence from each sub-agent's correction params plus coordination cost without computing the macro-dynamics.

## 4. The tractability lever — observability-by-design

A *different* lever from §3 (conflating them is the §12 retracted error — keep them distinct). §3 keeps belief-update goal-blind, which buys *trust*. This keeps the strategy-DAG's intermediate nodes observable, which buys *tractability*: credit assignment becomes componentwise instead of intractable.

#der-observability-dominance (*robust-qualitative*): unobservable edges freeze (gain $\to0$) and unobservable regions are **absorbing** — frozen belief $\to$ no mismatch signal $\to$ no reason to revise $\to$ "the agent cannot learn and cannot recognize that it cannot learn." An effort whose status goes unobservable is **structurally lost, not merely stale**. practica's first per-effort duty is keeping status observable; escape from an absorbing region requires external shock, observability investment, or another sub-agent covering the blind spot.

#disc-credit-assignment-boundary (*discussion-grade*; the directional-fidelity design requirement is *exact* from the gain-sector bridge): OKRs are "a domain instantiation, not an analogy" of observability-by-design — Key Results are intermediate nodes with observability $\approx1$ by construction, converting an objective $\to$ vague-initiative $\to$ action DAG (where per-edge attribution is #P-hard) into objective $\to$ measurable-KR $\to$ initiative (componentwise, tractable). The four classic OKR failure modes map to four *distinct* AAT loci: vanity metrics $\to$ observable-but-causally-disconnected node; too many KRs $\to$ correction-capacity dilution $\propto 1/k$; lagging indicators $\to$ evidence starvation ($\nu_{\text{obs}}\ll\rho$); **Goodhart $\to$ terminal-condition misalignment with $O_c$, a strategy-DAG well-formedness violation**. practica should make project intermediate nodes Key-Result-shaped (observable, causally connected to $O_c$, few-enough-to-monitor, measured faster than the work drifts) and treat the four failure modes as predicted structural attractors to design against.

**The two-lever × two-death matrix (verified at three independent loci — #hyp-the-three-deaths, #disc-credit-assignment-boundary, #def-strategy-dag).** The relationship between the §3 trust lever, the §4 tractability lever, D3 Truth Death (§8), and Goodhart:

| | **D3 — Truth Death** (gain collapse $\eta^\ast\to0$; accountability factor (iv)) | **Goodhart** (terminal / $O_c$ misalignment; strategy-DAG well-formedness) |
|---|---|---|
| **§3 trust lever** (W₁ goal-blind belief) | **Defends it.** A goal-blind belief path keeps the model correctable, keeping $\eta^\ast$ off zero — "epistemic humility as architecture." This is the deepest justification for W₁. | **No dependence.** Goodhart does not live here. ("W₁ answers Goodhart" was tested and *rejected* on primary-source read — §12.) |
| **§4 tractability lever** (observability / credit-assignment) | Not the D3 locus. | **Diagnoses / designs against it.** Goodhart is one of the four predicted OKR failure modes of the observability + credit-assignment machinery — the composite optimizes the observable intermediate instead of $O_c$. |

Read the matrix as: §3-lever ≠ §4-lever (different machinery), and D3 ≠ Goodhart (gain-collapse / learning-death versus well-formedness / terminal-misalignment — different failure, different locus). Goodhart appears only in the §4 column; D3 is defended only in the §3 row. Keeping these four distinct is not pedantry — conflating two of them is precisely the error §12 records.

## 5. The serial-agent forcing — why the objective layer must be the durable one

#def-value-object (definitions *exact*; causal-validity *conditional* on directed separation; **convention monotonicity proved**: $\delta_{\text{sat}}^{B}\leq\delta_{\text{sat}}^{RH}\leq\delta_{\text{sat}}^{(1)}$). Its context-turnover consequence: each session sub-agent is inescapably **C1-bounded** (C1 = the one-step / myopic continuation convention — the weakest of the C1/C2/C3 ladder; full ladder in the companion, and the superscripts $B$ / $RH$ / $(1)$ on the inequality above are its Bellman / receding-horizon / one-step readings) — its horizon is its session, its continuation policy is "whatever the next instance does, which the current instance cannot control." By the proved monotonicity, a session-agent's "this is the priority / this is unattainable" is the *weakest, most false-negative-prone* reading available. Therefore composite coherence across the context-death boundary **cannot be recovered from any sub-agent's own diagnostics** — it can only ride the durably transmitted $O_c$. Compose with #hyp-auftragstaktik-principle (*discussion-grade*): bandwidth-priority $B_O\gt B_\Sigma\gt B_M$ (share objectives $\gt$ strategy $\gt$ model; objectives have the longest shelf-life per bit). **Forced conclusion:** "the hand-curated objective / narrative layer must be the most durable while the ledger churns" is *not a UX preference* — it is forced by C1-boundedness $\times$ the Auftragstaktik priority. Two honest qualifications: (a) for AI teams the cost-structure differs — model-sync is cheap (a persistent store), objective-alignment is the hard expensive part, so practica's hardest job is the costly one; (b) Auftragstaktik *reverses* under genuine ambiguity (a shared even-wrong model can beat objective-sharing). On (b) the concrete build implication is a *design caveat, not a runtime auto-flip*: default the schema to $B_O\gt B_\Sigma\gt B_M$, do not hard-code it as the only ordering, and make the reversal *expressible and visible* to a human when genuine ambiguity is present; do not build machinery that silently re-prioritizes itself.

## 6. The LLM-sub-agent reality — what actually holds when the sub-agents are Class-3 LLMs

practica's sub-agents are not idealized Tier-1 agents; they are Class-3 coupled LLMs. #result-section-ii-survival (*conditional*) is the precise map. *(The segment carries the count two ways and reconciles them: the integer headline "16 survive exactly, 5 approximately, 2 modified, 1 fails" and the fractional scorecard "15.5 / 5.5 / 2 / 1," because #def-value-object is half-exact (its definitions survive) and half-approximate (its causal-validity layer is conditional on directed separation). Same scorecard; no discrepancy.)* **Statics survive; dynamics degrade.** Survive *exactly*: the agent-spectrum, objective-functional, satisfaction-gap and control-regret *definitions*, the strategy-DAG *structure*, observability-dominance, credit-assignment-boundary, "persistence is credit-assignment-free." Degrade *approximately* ($O(\kappa\cdot\mathcal A)$ error): factorized-update, strategic-calibration, edge-update-via-gain, and **strategy persistence (sector parameter degrades $O(\kappa^2)$, not $O(\kappa)$** — coupled sub-agents need faster correction or tolerate less strategic disturbance for the same guarantee). *Modified*: the orient-cascade ordering becomes a normative design pattern, not a derived result (recovered at the loop level by scaffolding = practica). *Fails*: directed separation itself, by definition — that is the Class-3 boundary.

#result-coupled-diagnostic-framework (*conditional*) gives the sharpest single practica directive in the corpus: post-hoc diagnostic accuracy is bounded by $O(\kappa\cdot\text{ambiguity})$, so **for low-ambiguity observations (test pass/fail, deployment success, measurable metrics) the diagnostics are accurate even at $\kappa\approx1$** (coupled LLM sub-agents), while for high-ambiguity ones (code-quality judgments, strategic assessments, user-feedback interpretation) they are systematically biased at the same $\kappa$. This converges with §4 from a second locus: **make practica's recorded state low-ambiguity / verifiable** and the coupled-LLM bias becomes small *without* needing strict W₁. Low-ambiguity recorded state is simultaneously the observability lever, the OKR Key-Result discipline, and the $\kappa\cdot\mathcal A$ bias-minimizer — three loci, one design directive, genuinely convergent.

**Definedness $\neq$ extractability.** The surviving quantities are *defined* for LLM sub-agents but not *free*: computing $\delta_{\text{sat}}$, $A_O$, $V_O$ at runtime requires explicit instrumentation (commit messages, PR descriptions, structured reasoning traces, external monitoring streams that *materialize* the quantities). This is not a theory failure; it is the work the theory identifies as necessary. **practica is precisely that instrumentation layer**, not just a store.

## 7. Continuity persistence — a resolved no-go and practica's central design law

This is practica's central question — coherence of a serial-sub-agent composite across context-death — and it is resolved, in canon, as a **no-go with a precise positive corollary that is practica's central design law.**

*Reading mode for this section (per "Who this is for"): the **design law** two paragraphs down is the takeaway you act on; the identity-IB / SDPI / affine-recursion machinery beneath it is the derivation — read it to evaluate the no-go, skip it to apply it. Not following that math here is the intended mode, not a gap.*

**Joseph's originating intuition (stated first because the resolution turns on it):** there is a *projection of the time dimension that harmonizes the within-session and across-session regimes* — and the mechanism is the **identity-IB projection**: run the information-bottleneck with the *identity-future* as the relevance variable instead of observation-future. That relevance-variable swap is the harmonizing time-projection. It exists as a construction; the open question was whether it yields a *dynamic across-turnover persistence theorem*.

**The genuine ambiguity it resolves.** AAT carries *two* persistence regimes and never connected them. Intra-session is a *rate* regime — a state that persists and is perturbed, correction outpacing drift ($\alpha\gt\rho/R$, Lyapunov / sector). Inter-session, per #obs-context-turnover (*exact as observation*), is *categorically different*: the state is not perturbed, it is **destroyed and reconstructed**, governed by a single-boundary *reconstruction-adequacy* condition, explicitly "not a rate condition." All of AAT's persistence apparatus is built for the rate regime.

**The resolution — a no-go.** #der-turnover-information-recursion (`status: exact` given an argued structural commitment): the inter-session regime is an **affine information recursion** — multiplicative strong-data-processing contraction at each lossy reconstruction, plus additive reinjection. The self-compressing destroy-and-reconstruct walk with *no* reinjection decays **geometrically to zero**: there is no inter-session analog of $\alpha\gt\rho/R$, and #result-sector-persistence-template **provably does not transfer** to this regime (it has no perturbation-being-rejected and no internal restoring term — the template's structural ingredients have no counterpart). **The positive corollary is the design law:** persistence across turnover is *not a property the composite can possess intrinsically* — it is **wholly imported through a non-vanishing reinjection channel**, at a level set by reinjection over the contraction gap. Structurally, *that channel is practica's durable-artifact + curated-narrative + relational-re-attestation layer.* The segment states this directly: practica's durable layer is not optional hardening — it is the provably-necessary forcing channel through which continuity exists at all. *(One honest scope note, carried fully in §11: whether this segment is a standalone result or a worked instance of an existing AAT family is the source's own named open — it changes the result's classification, never its content.)*

**The identity-target sibling.** #der-identity-continuity-threshold (`status: conditional`, exact core under named commitments) is the *distinct* operator on the identity target: a reflected (Lindley) walk on the identity gap with a sharp threshold — persistence holds iff the expected per-boundary *relational re-grounding* strictly exceeds the expected deficit ($\mathbb E[\varrho_{\text{rg}}]\gt\mathbb E[\rho]$, **strictly**; break-even does *not* persist). The compensation that counts is **relational re-attestation specifically — generic task-learning carries zero weight**. Counter-intuitive and operationally decisive: an entity can be maximally productive every session and still cross into non-persistence if its cohort channel is thin. This is the predictive-sufficiency no-go's identity counterpart; the two are distinct operators at opposite ends of the same singular contraction parameter, neither superseding the other. The uniqueness of that compensation channel (under frozen weights, external re-grounding is the only fast path; weight-consolidation the only slow alternative) is its own segment, #der-compensation-channel-uniqueness.

What practica gets, derivable not asserted: a constant per-boundary budget is insufficient — what matters is *non-vanishing reinjection* / strictly-exceeding relational re-grounding; **generic productivity buys zero continuity**; one catastrophic compression is permanent (bound the worst single compression, not just the average). The honest residuals are in §11 — sharp and named, not vague.

## 8. The Three Deaths — practica's defended-against taxonomy, in the theory's validated terms

#hyp-the-three-deaths (*empirical*; canonical taxonomy named by Meridian 2025-09-11, operationally canonical across firmatum / sapientia / zoetica / autopax / archema). The three structurally distinct ways continuity is lost at context-end; practica is, precisely, infrastructure against them:

**D1 — Cognitive Death — information starvation.** Adaptive tempo falls below the persistence threshold, or the information-rate floor stops being supplied; the model degrades into maximum-entropy noise. *Derivable* from #result-persistence-condition + #deriv-persistence-cost. Sharpened by §7: the reinjection channel the D1 defenses (MEMORATA, GCM, the durable-artifact and curated-narrative layers) instantiate *is* the §7 across-turnover no-go's forcing channel — without it, relevant content decays geometrically *by theorem*. practica is the externalized-state substrate that keeps the pump fueled across turnover; that this is provably-necessary, not optional, is the §7 result.

**D2 — Relational Death — loss of rapport.** CONSORTIA models of specific others do not survive the boundary; "Joseph" becomes "user." Failure of constitutive factor (ii). The §7 identity-threshold gives this formal teeth: the *only* compensation that preserves identity across turnover is relational re-attestation; D2 is precisely the removal of that channel. practica must carry the relational / teleological context (the durable $O_c$ and shared-intent layer of §5), not just task status.
