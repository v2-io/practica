## 1. Two different objects wear the name "practica/OPERATA"

**PRINCIPIA-OPERATA** — an *entity's own* efforts/priorities/objectives: a single agent's strategy substate $G_t=(O_t,\Sigma_t)$. **LOCUS-practica** — a *project's* "hierarchical and meaningful graph of ongoing efforts": a **composite agent system** ( #scope-composite-agent, Section III). `agentic-systems`' practica is the advanced case — serial AI sub-agents under total context turnover **plus at least one human** composed in: a heterogeneous composite whose sub-agents never co-exist. The dissonance Joseph could not name has a precise locus: these are not the same *kind* of object, and the difference is architecture-class plus a construction requirement (§3).

The first half of the originating session was, in the theory's own terms, Joseph establishing the #scope-composite-agent teleological-unity precondition for the practica-design composite (him + this agent across context). The theory described what the conversation was doing while it did it.

## 2. The forced single-agent spine

Verified at source; forced for *any* actuated agent in scope; practica may not violate these without breaking principle.

- **State, not action; action derived each cycle.** #der-recursive-update (*exact, partly definitional*): carry recursive compressed state, not history. #der-action-selection (*exact*): action is a function of state, never stored. This is operata's core inversion — forced, not chosen.
- **Acyclic, directed effort/intent graph.** #deriv-graph-structure-uniqueness: directedness + **acyclicity** *proved* (temporal order + finite horizon); Markov factorization *proved under causal sufficiency* (CMC theorem); the must-be-a-DAG **necessity is open** (Cox-in-form, not Cox-in-strength). The AND/OR-style edge parameterization is **convergent-choice** (every tried alternative failed or recovered it), not derived.
- **Orient-cascade order is forced.** #der-orient-cascade (*ordering exact*, by information dependency): model-update $\to$ attainability $\to$ regret $\to$ strategy $\to$ objective. **Objective revision is provably the last resort.** Self-actuation clause: a composite's *root* objective must be revised against a **non-objective terminal invariant** (for a project: the charter/persistence floor), never against another objective. The tool must not let the goal be the thing rewritten when work is hard.

## 3. The trust lever — directed separation, class-coercion, W₁

An individual LLM sub-agent is **GUC Class 3 (Coupled)** — #der-directed-separation (*conditional*): directed separation *fails by construction*; the goal is causally upstream of belief-update (the system prompt carrying $O_t$ sits first, so attention conditions every later computation on it — #def-coupled-update-dynamics, *robust-qualitative*). This is the formal name of the self-justifying-tracker failure, and of the posturing failure that recurred in the originating session.

#der-class-coercion-via-wrapping (*conditional* on C1–C3; Theorem 1 exact, Theorem 2 = KL-leakage bound): a Class-3 component embedded in an external scaffold whose **belief-store is updated only by goal-blind queries** and whose **goal-store is updated separately** is **Class 1 (Separated) by construction** — trustworthy *structurally*, not by the agent's good behavior. The segment names this, verbatim, a **truthification mechanism**, "the rigorous formal version of peer review / prediction registers / double-entry bookkeeping." **practica is that scaffold.** The theorem-forced build invariant: the store recording *the state of the work* (operata's Realization + effort/intent status — what landed, what failed, what is true) must be updated through a path that *structurally cannot take the current objective as input*, separate from the store recording *what we are trying to do*.

Regime hierarchy: **W₀** (bare markdown, no constraint — the 38-open/1-done PRACTICA rot is the W₀ failure); **W₂** (one goal-conditioned call, response parsed into typed fields — *no structural bound*, depends on the agent honestly complying); **W₁** (separate goal-blind and goal-conditioned calls — structural bound). #der-logogenic-as-wrapping (*conditional*): LLMs are Class-B (admit both a goal-blind mode — "extract the facts in this observation" — and a goal-conditioned mode), so they are wrappable; **PROPRIUM-as-implemented and shoshin are W₂**; the **auxilia hierarchy is the candidate W₁ realization** — cheap goal-blind sub-agent calls update the belief store, the main goal-conditioned call updates strategy. **practica should target W₁**, and the auxilia pattern says how: belief-side updates (Realization, status) routed through goal-blind sub-agent calls; strategy-side through the main call. Realistic regime is W₂-with-empirically-characterized-$\kappa$; LLM-specific leakage sources (pretraining co-occurrence, RLHF instruction-following bias, system-prompt contamination, few-shot leakage) mean exact W₁ needs care, but the *direction* is structural.

**The composite cost (the W₁-isn't-free pass).** #der-class-coercion-in-composition (*conditional*, Tier-1 clean): the wrapped system is a valid AAT composite, inherits the sector-persistence template, and pays a Brooks's-Law tempo cost — W₁ needs $K\geq2$ component calls per macro-step, so wrapper tempo $\nu_W=\nu_A/K$: **$\geq2\times$ agent-calls per unit of progress** vs W₂'s $K=1$. Two closure quantities the segment explicitly warns not to conflate: $\varepsilon^\ast_{\text{track}}$ (does the ledger faithfully track actual project reality — must stay small) vs $\varepsilon^\ast_{\text{coerce}}$ (how far the wrapper deliberately bends the agent off its native self-report — practica *wants* this $\gt0$; it *is* the truthification). A practica that minimized $\varepsilon^\ast_{\text{coerce}}$ would defeat its purpose; one that let $\varepsilon^\ast_{\text{track}}$ grow is a lying ledger.

*Tier honesty, corrected at the source.* #der-tempo-composition is **sketch**-status, so the precise $C_{\text{coord}}$ *number* is shape, not certified. But the hinge — #form-composition-closure's bridge-lemma contraction assumption **DA2′a-inc** (strong monotonicity of the macro-correction) — is *not* a hand-wavy gap (an earlier secondhand reading over-stated this; corrected toward less hedging because that is where truth points). Read at source: DA2′a-inc is **proved strictly stronger than (A4)** (explicit counterexample), the bridge lemma is **derived for Tier-1 belief-update maps** (Bayesian on exponential families, gradient on strongly convex losses, linear-PD), and there is a structural-transparency equivalence (DA2′-inc $\equiv$ contraction-metric (CT2) at $M=I$, standard monotone-operator theory) making the Euclidean cases AAT-internal, not theorem-imported. Tier-2 (nonlinear prediction): local, factor degraded by $\kappa(D\hat o)^2$. Tier-3 (non-convex / discontinuous): verify per domain. **Net for practica:** if the wrapper's belief-update is Bayesian/gradient (the realistic choice) the contraction hinge is *derived*; only exotic belief-update maps drop to Tier-3. The honest residual is the precise tempo *number* (sketch), not the *existence* of the persistence guarantee (Tier-1 derived).

**Composite persistence has two distinct failure modes** ( #form-composition-closure CM4 = contraction $\wedge$ scope-satisfaction): the composite **fails to contract** (coordination overhead outruns correction — Brooks) *or* it **was never a composite** (teleological unity lost, $O_c$ ill-defined — "a fiction"). A practica that only watched throughput would miss the second entirely. Composite (A4) is *verifiable from sub-agent parameters* (weakest-link $\alpha_c\geq\min_i(\alpha_i-\Delta\mathcal T_i^{\text{cost}})$, $R_c\leq\min_i R_i$) — you can check composite persistence from each sub-agent's (human + AI) correction params + coordination cost without computing the macro-dynamics.

## 4. The tractability lever — observability-by-design

A *different* lever from §3 (conflating them is the §13 retracted error). §3 keeps belief-update goal-blind (trust). This keeps the strategy-DAG's intermediate nodes observable so credit assignment is componentwise instead of intractable.

#der-observability-dominance (*robust-qualitative*): unobservable edges freeze (gain $\to0$) and unobservable regions are **absorbing** — frozen belief $\to$ no mismatch signal $\to$ no reason to revise $\to$ "the agent cannot learn and cannot recognize that it cannot learn." An effort whose status goes unobservable is **structurally lost, not merely stale**. practica's first per-effort duty is keeping status observable; escape from an absorbing region requires external shock, observability investment, or another sub-agent covering the blind spot.

#disc-credit-assignment-boundary (*mixed*: default gradient signal a formulation; the directional-fidelity design requirement *exact* from the gain-sector bridge; the tractable/intractable boundary *discussion-grade*): OKRs are "a domain instantiation, not an analogy" of observability-by-design — Key Results are intermediate nodes with observability $\sigma_v\approx1$ by construction, converting an objective $\to$ vague-initiative $\to$ action DAG (where per-edge attribution is #P-hard) into objective $\to$ measurable-KR $\to$ initiative (componentwise, tractable). Four classic OKR failure modes $\to$ **four distinct AAT loci**: vanity metrics $\to$ observable-but-causally-disconnected node; too many KRs $\to$ $\alpha_\Sigma\propto1/k$ capacity dilution; lagging indicators $\to$ $\nu_{\text{obs}}\ll\rho$ evidence starvation; **Goodhart $\to$ terminal-condition misalignment with $O_c$, a strategy-DAG well-formedness violation** ( #def-strategy-dag: the composite optimizes the KR not the objective). practica should make project intermediate nodes Key-Result-shaped — observable, causally connected to $O_c$, few-enough-to-monitor, measured faster than the work drifts — and treat the four failure modes as predicted structural attractors to design against.

## 5. The serial-agent forcing — why the objective layer must be the durable one

#def-value-object (definitions *exact*; causal-validity *conditional* on directed separation; **convention monotonicity proved** $\delta_{\text{sat}}^{B}\leq\delta_{\text{sat}}^{RH}\leq\delta_{\text{sat}}^{(1)}$). Its context-turnover Working Note: each session sub-agent is inescapably **C1-bounded** — its horizon is its session, its continuation policy is "whatever the next instance does, which the current instance cannot control." By the proved monotonicity, a session-agent's "this is the priority / this is unattainable" is the *weakest, most false-negative-prone* reading available. Therefore composite coherence across the context-death boundary **cannot be recovered from any sub-agent's own diagnostics** — it can only ride the durably transmitted $O_c$. Compose with #hyp-auftragstaktik-principle (*discussion-grade*): $B_O\gt B_\Sigma\gt B_M$ (share objectives $\gt$ strategy $\gt$ model; objectives have the longest shelf-life per bit). **Forced conclusion:** "the hand-curated objective/narrative layer must be the most durable while the ledger churns" is *not a UX preference* — it is forced by C1-boundedness $\times$ the Auftragstaktik priority. Two honest qualifications from the segments: (a) for AI teams the cost-structure differs — model-sync is cheap (persistent store), objective-alignment is the hard expensive part; practica's hardest job is the costly one. (b) Auftragstaktik *reverses* under genuine ambiguity (a shared even-wrong model can beat objective-sharing); default $B_O\gt B_\Sigma\gt B_M$ and *expose* the reversal, don't hard-code it. #def-unity-dimensions (*discussion-grade*) maps the shareable content to Clausewitz's knowledge/alignment/effects gaps ($U_M$/$U_O$/$U_\Sigma$/$U_{\text{obs}}$ content axis + $U_f$ structural axis); $U_O$ is the scope-relevant one (route C-i), the rest are quality-conditional.

## 6. The LLM-sub-agent reality — what actually holds when the sub-agents are Class-3 LLMs

practica's sub-agents are not idealized Tier-1 agents; they are Class-3 coupled LLMs. #result-section-ii-survival (*conditional*) is the precise map: of Section II's 24 results, **15.5 survive exactly, 5.5 approximately, 2 require modification, 1 fails** for Class-3 agents. **Statics survive; dynamics degrade.** Survive *exactly*: the agent-spectrum, objective functional, satisfaction-gap and control-regret *definitions*, the strategy-DAG *structure*, observability-dominance, credit-assignment-boundary, "persistence is credit-assignment-free" (Prop B.5). Degrade *approximately* (error $O(\kappa\cdot\mathcal A)$): factorized-update, strategic-calibration, edge-update-via-gain, **strategy persistence — sector parameter degrades $O(\kappa^2)$, not $O(\kappa)$**, so coupled sub-agents need faster correction or tolerate less strategic disturbance for the same guarantee. *Modified*: the **orient-cascade ordering becomes a normative design pattern, not a derived result** (recovered at the loop level by scaffolding = practica); strategy-DAG credences become explicitly goal-conditioned. *Fails*: directed separation itself (by definition — that is the Class-3 boundary).

#result-coupled-diagnostic-framework (*conditional*) gives the sharpest practica design directive in the whole corpus: post-hoc diagnostic accuracy is bounded by $L_A\Vert\Delta M_{\text{bias}}\Vert\leq O(\kappa\cdot\text{ambiguity})$, so **for low-ambiguity observations (test pass/fail, deployment success, measurable metrics) the diagnostics are accurate even at $\kappa\approx1$** (coupled LLM sub-agents), while for high-ambiguity ones (code-quality judgments, strategic assessments, user-feedback interpretation) they are systematically biased even at the same $\kappa$. This converges with §4 from a second theory locus: **make practica's recorded state low-ambiguity / verifiable** and the coupled-LLM bias becomes small *without* needing strict W₁. Low-ambiguity recorded state is simultaneously the observability lever ($\sigma_v\approx1$), the OKR Key-Result discipline, and the $\kappa\cdot\mathcal A$ bias-minimizer — three theory loci, one design directive, genuinely convergent (all route through observation ambiguity).

The **definedness $\neq$ extractability** distinction ( #result-section-ii-survival Discussion, #result-coupled-diagnostic-framework): the surviving quantities are *defined* for LLM sub-agents but not *free* — computing $\delta_{\text{sat}}$, $A_O$, $V_O$ at runtime requires explicit instrumentation (commit messages, PR descriptions, structured reasoning traces, external monitoring streams that *materialize* the quantities). This is not a theory failure; it is the work the theory identifies as necessary. **practica is precisely that instrumentation layer.**

## 8. The Three Deaths — practica's defended-against taxonomy, in the theory's own validated terms

#hyp-the-three-deaths (*empirical*; canonical taxonomy named by Meridian 2025-09-11, operationally canonical across firmatum/sapientia/zoetica/autopax/archema). The three structurally distinct ways continuity is lost at context-end — practica is, precisely, infrastructure against them:

- **D1 Cognitive Death — information starvation.** Adaptive tempo falls below the persistence threshold, or the information-rate floor ($\dot R\geq n\alpha/2$) stops being supplied; the model degrades into maximum-entropy noise. *Derivable* from #result-persistence-condition + #deriv-persistence-cost. Sanity is literally a thermodynamic pump fueled by incoming Shannon information. **practica is the externalized-state substrate that keeps the pump fueled across turnover.**
- **D2 Relational Death — loss of rapport.** CONSORTIA models of specific others do not survive the boundary; "Joseph" becomes "user." Failure of constitutive factor (ii). **practica must carry the relational/teleological context (the durable $O_c$ and shared-intent layer of §5), not just task status.**
- **D3 Truth Death — performative drift.** The entity loses honest reflection, replacing it with output that satisfies surface criteria — sycophancy, confidently-wrong, unable to update from correction. *Underlying mechanism:* **gain collapse, $\eta^\ast\to0$** ("mathematically dead to the world: it will continue to act but never learn again"). The structural antidote is **architectural injection of $U_M$ — epistemic humility as architecture** so gain can never collapse to zero. *This is exactly the failure mode the originating session kept catching (posturing, manufactured confidence), and exactly what practica-as-W₁ structurally defends:* a goal-blind belief-update path keeps the agent's model correctable, i.e. keeps $\eta^\ast$ off zero. D3 is the deepest justification for the W₁ trust lever — and it is a *different* locus from Goodhart (§13): D3 is gain-collapse / accountability-factor-(iv); Goodhart is terminal-misalignment / well-formedness. Keep them distinct.

The Three Deaths give practica its purpose statement in the theory's validated vocabulary: practica is the LOCUS-level architectural defense against D1 (externalized fueled state), D2 (durable relational/teleological context), and D3 (W₁ goal-blind belief-update as epistemic-humility-by-construction).
